I'm Dr. Steve Kelman.

I'm a Professor of Public Management at Harvard University.

Under the Clinton Administration, I served as the Chief Procurement Officer of the U.S. federal government.

I've researched and authored dozens of publications (books, articles, etc.) related to public management, with an emphasis on organizational management and procurement.

I regularly write for Federal Computer Week, covering a range of topics, including public-sector procurement innovation.

I serve as an advisor or board member to nearly a dozen organizations, including the National Contract Management Association.

I also advise public-sector leaders on innovative procurement models and practices, as well as organizational management.

If you think I can be of service, please contact me via email, LinkedIn, or Twitter.
